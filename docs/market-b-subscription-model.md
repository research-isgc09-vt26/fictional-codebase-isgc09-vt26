# Market B subscription model

Market B can right now use only the `CommunityOnlySubscriptionModel`.
Waiting on answer from legal, when other subscription types can be launched.

NB! Stakeholders from Market B refer to the community membership product as just "subscription"!
Market B does not offer online-only subscription.
Market B offers `MembershipSignup` from offline events, but without the standard 30-day free trial — the signup is registered immediately and uses a market-specific customer message.
