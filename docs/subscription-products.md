# Subscription products

SkillStar Learning company has launched its on-line learning platform and plans a series of subscription products with various level of access.

Currently launched subscription products:
- `OnlineSubscription` - gives access to the online platform.
- `CommunityMembershipSubscription` - includes online platform access and community membership, where you can get access for onsite workshops, conferences and community events.
Community membership may require additional profile fields for local events, communication, accessibility needs, emergency contact preferences, and membership administration.
- `MembershipSignup` - offline-first access with possibility to get access to online resources as a paid add-on after a trial
